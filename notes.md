* `RACE std models` are models trained over the complete dataset, unmodified.
* `QUAIL std models` same
* `QUAIL mod models` are trained over a modified version of the dataset, where all questions whose correct answer is "not enough information" are removed.

Empty answer corrections refer to deciding not to answer, which is different
than answering "not enough information"

No answer refers to answering the option "not enough information"

Unless stated otherwise, prediction corrections are done by deciding not to answer
